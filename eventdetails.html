Event Description
Unique in its kind, wooden puzzle "Bear's Dream". Each detail of the puzzle is unique and is part of a hidden plot, which opens as you collect the whole picture. You will learn what the bear dreamed during hibernation. You will find here such characters as a fox, a wolf, a raccoon and even a big barrel of honey. The wooden two-step puzzle "Bear's Dream" is an imaginary story about what a bear sees in a dream during hibernation. And he sees a vivid dream in which there are images of forest animals: owls, wolves, bats, barrels of honey, deer, roe deer, golden eagle, mushrooms, foxes, turtles, squirrels and other characters. In addition, this puzzle has a double puzzle. In the puzzle itself there is an image of a bear, which consists of separate parts. You will have fun and interesting.
